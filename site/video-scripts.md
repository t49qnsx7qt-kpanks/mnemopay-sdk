## Production Notes

**For all videos:**
- Record 5-min Digital Twin source video first (good lighting, continuous take)
- Use HeyGen Creator plan ($24/mo) for unlimited generation
- Generate each Avatar Shot (15 sec clips), stitch 2-3 together per video
- Add text overlays + code snippets in CapCut (free)
- Export at 1080x1920 (vertical) for Reels/TikTok/Shorts
- Export at 1920x1080 (horizontal) for LinkedIn/YouTube
- Add captions — 80% of social video is watched on mute
